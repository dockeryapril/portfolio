About Me

I began my Creative journey about 13 years ago, obtaining my degree in Multi-Age Education and becoming an art teacher. I transitioned my skills into graphic design and photography, which, naturally, led me into art directing, branding, marketing, and campaign management. I've worked with a huge variety of clients. From first-time entrepreneurs to small local businesses, big name brands and even mid-sized financial institutions. I'm finally adding front-end web development to my skillset as I love evolving in the tech and business space.

My interest in business and e-commerce led to a passion for developing on the Shopify platform, which I've been doing consistently for over 6 years as a Shopify Partner.

I absolutely love the outdoors, especially fishing, so much so that I started my own fishing brand, The Reel Plug Fishing Co. And, of course, it's powered by Shopify.

Projects

The Reel Plug Fishing Co.

The Reel Plug Fishing Co. provides convenient fishing and outdoor gear ranging from our own eco-conscious apparel line and thrifted goods to carefully curated fishing gear and collabs with other small fishing businesses.

Built on Shopify, dynamic content was added to the site with the theme's default pages using simple HTML to utilize a plugin that offers TRP fisherfolx interactive maps for locating places to fish and boat.

I'm currently working on building a marketplace app to extend the existing functionality of what The Reel Plug offers. The Bait & Lure Library is a Shopify app that will be available as a Shopify channel to connect multiple small business baitmakers already on the Shopify platform with other fisherfolx looking to purchase handcrafted and custom baits - all in one marketplace online.
