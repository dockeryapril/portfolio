About Me

I began my Creative journey about 13 years ago, obtaining my degree in Multi-Age Education and becoming an art teacher. I transitioned my skills into graphic design and photography, which, naturally, led me into art directing, branding, digital marketing, and even campaign management. I've worked with a huge variety of clients. From first-time entrepreneurs to small local businesses, big name brands and even mid-sized financial institutions. I'm finally adding front-end web development to my skillset as I love the evolving and challenging tech space.

My interest in business and e-commerce led to a passion for developing on the Shopify platform, which I've been doing consistently for over 6 years as a Shopify Partner.

I absolutely love the outdoors, especially fishing, so much so that I started my own fishing brand, The Reel Plug Fishing Co. And, of course, it's powered by Shopify.
