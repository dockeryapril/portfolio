About Me

I began my Creative journey 13 years ago, obtaining a degree in Multi-Age Education to become an art teacher and freelance illustrator. I transitioned my creative skills into graphic design and photography. This led to more opportunities to actually utilize my observational skills and limited psychology knowledge, obtained from teaching, into designing user experiences and interfaces for a variety of clients and industries. From designing logos and branding guides for first-time entrepreneurs and small local businesses. To working with the creative briefs of big name consumer brands and running multi-channel campaigns for financial institutions with millions in assets. My experience is solidified with an adaptable and evolving skillset and dedication to design and development principles that give me the confidence to push boundaries and get results.

Developing on Shopify allows me to build on their lo-fi wireframe templates into interactive prototypes to a live site all on one platform, while bridging my training from UX/UI to front-end wed development.

REINVENTION FITNESS, LLC

Reinvention Fitness is a fitness brand on the Shopify platform currently using the Boundless theme along with various simple HTML and CSS implementations. The brand is currently pivoting in response to the impacts of covid. A custom theme is currently being built utilizing Dawn, Shopify's reference theme. The approach is keep it minimal while using strong imagery and video for storytelling. Even with gyms reopening, a whole new culture has emerged as virtual workouts take center stage and more people that are "gym-shy" realize that the world is their gym. Featuring real people, real stories, and real solutions. This is still an e-comm site, so this mobile-first theme minimizing complexities that can hinder purchasing decisions for fitness products while still giving powerful performance for a verity of virtual class options which include integrations with Facebook and the Metaverse.

ROBO-FISHFRIENDS

A quick-build, dynamic React-based project created for my three-year-old nephew to keep track of the types of fish he catches while learning the craft itself. Each robo-fish friend card is coded into the app every time a new species is caught. The robo-fish characters are randomly drawn into the app via the robohash.org API.
